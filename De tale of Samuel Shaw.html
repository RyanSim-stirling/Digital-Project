Tale of Samuel Shaw

In 2010, a student named Samuel Shaw was found dead in one of the old campus bathrooms. Official reports said it was an overdose — methamphetamine. They claimed he was using it to cope with the intense pressure of school, two part-time jobs, and a fractured home life.

But the story didn’t end there.

Ever since his death, strange things have been happening in that bathroom. A sharp, bitter chemical smell clings to the air — artificial, burning.

Then there’s the laughter. Soft. Breathy. Wrong. Not joyful — the sound of someone laughing to themselves in the dark.

Some say the laughter can be heard from outside, even when the bathroom is empty. If you try to enter, it stops… but not always.

One janitor reportedly quit mid-shift after unlocking the stall during routine cleaning. They never said what they saw. Just left the keys on the floor and walked out.

Some believe Samuel’s spirit is trapped. Others think he returned to spread his sorrow. Either way, no one uses that bathroom after sunset.
